Sjoerd De Jong also known as Hourences is a Dutch Belgian video game developer level designer and the founder of Teotl Studios He serves as technology evangelist for Epic Games promoting Unreal Engine in the northern half of Europe Career De Jong started making levels for video games at the age of when he purchased a copy of Unreal in Shortly after he started to make a name for himself in the Unreal community not only for the many maps he released but also for his work on popular Unreal Tournament mods such as Operation Na Pali Xidia and Jailbreak With a few more custom maps and contributions to mods for Unreal Tournament he was contracted by Epic Games to create maps for Unreal Tournament two of which were included in the demo He has worked on several games including Killzone The Chronicles of Spellborn and Huxley In March following his work on Syndicate he founded Teotl Studios The studio s first project was the first person action adventure game The Ball which was originally a mod for Unreal Tournament His most recent work The Solus Project came out in Video game credits Unreal Tournament Epic Games level designer artist Street Racing Syndicate Eutechnyx D artist Shellshock Nam Guerrilla Games environment artist Killzone Guerrilla Games Environment artist Warpath Digital Extremes level designer artist The Chronicles of Spellborn Spellborn International senior level artist Huxley Webzen Games level designer artist The Ball Teotl Studios creative director project lead Q U B E Toxic Games lighting material artist Syndicate Starbreeze Studios senior level designer artist Unmechanical Talawa Games creative director project lead Rekoil Plastic Piranha level artist The Solus Project Teotl Studios creative director project lead Books The Hows and Whys of the Games Industry Self published The Hows and Whys of Level Design Self published References External links Official website
