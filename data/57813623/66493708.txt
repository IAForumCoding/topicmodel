domestic simulcast with several other ViacomCBS cable networks and was broadcast live or tape delayed across all of Nickelodeon s international networks The ceremony was held with most presenters winners and performers in person with some appearing virtually due to the ongoing COVID pandemic The show also featured an interactive fan wall to allow for an audience and utilized XR technology
