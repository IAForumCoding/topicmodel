title: Seek and Destroy (1996 video game)
id: 26478784
Seek and Destroy is a Amiga action video game developed by Vision Software and published by Mindscape and released in for the Amiga CD A different version was also developed for MS DOS by the same developer and released in by publisher Safari Software then a division of Epic MegaGames The game bears resemblance to Desert Strike Return to the Gulf Reception The Amiga version of Seek and Destroy was well received with positive reviews from Amiga Format Amiga Power CU Amiga and The One References External links Seek and Destroy at MobyGames
