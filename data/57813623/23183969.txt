Develop was a monthly UK trade magazine for the video game industry Its online portal complete with a digital version of the print publication was active since July Develop was an annual rating system for game developers produced by Develop It was a ranking of the world s games development studios based on a variety of criteria including sales data critical success and industry standing The Develop Industry Excellence Awards also annually honoured the development of video games and award studios across a range of categories In November NewBay Media the owner of Develop at the time announced that the websites magazines and events of Develop and sister magazine Esports Pro would be absorbed into MCV by early with the combined magazine moving to a monthly frequency References External links Official website archived
