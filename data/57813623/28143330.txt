VG previously videogaming is a video game blog published in the United Kingdom founded in February by industry veteran Patrick Garratt Its current Editor in Chief is Dom Peppiatt In CNET ranked it as the third best gaming blog in the world History Founded in collaboration on February between games journalist Patrick Garratt and Eurogamer Network VG was set up to be a news only blog the first of its kind in the UK to have a specialist games blog found among the likes of American sites Kotaku and Joystiq At launch VG did not review video games and focused instead on news interviews and previews Garratt was the only staff member at the time of launch although grew in time with the addition of contributors Mike Bowden and Nathan Grayson In early the site relaunched itself rebranding from videogaming com to VG at the same time the site changed its primary url to www vg com and launched a new site design with improved features and staff The site added additional staff over the next year VG began to create original video content in and had some staff changes Matt Martin became the site s editor in chief and website founder Patrick Garratt moved into a full time publisher role in in the same period the website launched its third design iteration The site launched an Italian edition in March In October VG pulled their preview of Uncharted A Thief s End and issued an apology after being contacted by Sony that the game they had played was Uncharted Among Thieves from the remastered Uncharted The Nathan Drake Collection In February ReedPOP acquired the Gamer Network making VG a subsidiary of RELX Group Starting in September the site started to add reviews for recent games using a star ratings system In May VG started its first podcast titled The Best Games Ever Podcast In May the Gamer Network was sold to IGN Entertainment making VG a subsidiary of Ziff Davis Awards VG has been nominated twice for Best Website and Best Online Blog at the Games Media Awards in and While it did not win either award in in it won Best Online Blog with Garratt winning Best Specialist Online Writer Garratt was awarded three Games Media Legend awards in The site was nominated for the
